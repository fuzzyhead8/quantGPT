Brief strategy explanation: The Ichimoku strategy uses the Ichimoku Cloud indicator, which consists of five lines (Tenkan-sen, Kijun-sen, Senkou Span A, Senkou Span B, and Chikou Span) to determine the direction of the trend, support and resistance levels, and potential buy/sell signals. The strategy involves buying when the price crosses above the cloud or when the Tenkan-sen crosses above the Kijun-sen. Conversely, the strategy includes selling when the price crosses below the cloud or when the Tenkan-sen crosses below the Kijun-sen.
